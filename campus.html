Vinayak Law College

The Moot Court
— Where Lawyers Are Made

The crown jewel of Vinayak Law College's infrastructure is its state-of-the-art, purpose-built Moot Court — not a standard classroom, but a meticulously designed facility that replicates the exact spatial, procedural, and psychological environment of Indian judicial courts.

This high-pressure training arena forces law students to develop the skills that no textbook can teach: real-time argumentation, cross-examination technique, legal research under pressure, and the unshakeable confidence of a seasoned litigator.

  • Court Architecture: Replicates Judge's bench, witness box, counsels' podiums, and gallery seating of actual district courts
  • Mock Trials: Regular case-based mock cross-examinations and adversarial debate sessions
  • Judiciary Interaction: Management-facilitated sessions with sitting Rajasthan High Court judges
  • Court Visit Programmes: Mandatory visits to district courts and Rajasthan High Court, Jaipur
  • Moot Court Competitions: Inter-college and intra-college competitive mooting rounds
  • Legal Research Library: Integrated library with All India Reporter (AIR) volumes and current law journals
DBT Star College

Advanced Laboratories
& Research Centers

As a designated DBT Star College under the Department of Biotechnology, Government of India, Vinayak International College has invested heavily in research-grade laboratory infrastructure that goes far beyond standard undergraduate requirements.

These laboratories foster a culture of scientific inquiry from the undergraduate level itself — producing graduates who are genuinely research-ready, equipped for competitive examinations and advanced study.

  • Chemistry Labs: Wet and dry labs with modern analytical instruments — spectrophotometers, centrifuges, chromatography setups
  • Biology Labs: Dedicated Botany and Zoology labs with microscopy facilities and specimen collections
  • Physics Labs: Complete experimental physics setups for standard and advanced UG experiments
  • Computer Lab: Well-equipped computing facility with internet access for digital learning
  • Research Support: DBT Star funding enables equipment upgrades and research project facilitation
  • Safety Standards: Certified fume hoods, safety cabinets, and lab-grade personal protection equipment
Physical Excellence

Military Training,
Yoga & Sports

At Vinayak Group, physical fitness is not an extracurricular afterthought — it is a core curricular mandate. Every student participates in a structured physical conditioning programme that the management believes is essential for producing stress-free, resilient, and highly competitive graduates.

The integration of military-style training distinguishes Vinayak graduates from those of conventional colleges. The discipline, physical endurance, and mental fortitude built through these programmes directly translate to superior performance in government competitive examinations.

  • Military-Style Physical Training: Structured morning drills, discipline exercises, and physical endurance training under qualified instructors
  • Mandatory Yoga Programme: Daily / regular Yoga sessions focused on stress management, flexibility, and mental wellbeing
  • Scout & Guide Programme: Character-building civic training aligned with the Bharat Scout and Guide framework
  • Vast Open Sports Grounds: Expansive athletic tracks and multipurpose outdoor sports areas
  • Multipurpose Indoor Hall: Large hall for indoor events, gymnastics, and Yoga sessions
  • Swimming Pool: Olympic-standard pool at the International School campus
  • Indoor Gymnasium: Fully equipped gym for strength and conditioning training
