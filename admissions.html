Scholarships & Financial Aid

The Vinayak Group actively facilitates access to state and national scholarship programmes. Multiple opportunities exist for deserving students.

🌟 Kalibai Bheel Medhavi Chhatra Scooty Yojana
Rajasthan Government's flagship scheme for meritorious female students. Vinayak students have a strong track record of securing this award (e.g., students scoring 84%+ in B.Sc. have successfully received this benefit).
📚 National Scholarship Portal (NSP)
Central Sector Scholarship for College & University students; Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST/OBC students. Apply at scholarships.gov.in. Institution facilitates the application process.
🏛️ Rajasthan State Scholarship Portal
State-level scholarships for economically weaker sections, backward classes, and girl students. Access via rajpms.nic.in. Vinayak actively assists eligible students in applications.
📋 SC/ST Government Fee Concession
Full or partial fee waiver for students belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes as per Rajasthan Government norms. Requires valid caste certificate.
💡 Chief Minister Higher Education Scholarship
Mukhyamantri Uchch Shiksha Chhatravriti Yojana for BPL / economically weaker families pursuing higher education in Rajasthan.
🎓 OBC/EBC Scholarship
Other Backward Classes and Extremely Backward Classes scholarships through the Social Justice Department, Government of Rajasthan.

Scholarship Tip: Top-performing female students in B.Sc. programmes from Vinayak P.G. College have historically secured the Kalibai Bheel Medhavi Chhatra Scooty Yojana. Maintain your academic performance — it opens doors to both state recognition and financial support.
