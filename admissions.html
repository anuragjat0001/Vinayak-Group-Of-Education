Checklist

Documents Required
for Verification

  • Class 10 (Secondary) Marksheet + Certificate (Original + 2 Photocopies)
  • Class 12 (Senior Secondary) Marksheet + Certificate (Original + 2 Photocopies)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from last institution
  • Character Certificate from previous institution or Gazetted Officer
  • Aadhar Card (Original + 2 Photocopies)
  • Passport-size photographs (6 copies, recent)
  • Caste Certificate (for SC/ST/OBC — if applying for reservation benefits)
  • Income Certificate (for scholarship applications)
  • LL.B. only: Graduation Degree/Provisional Certificate + CLAT/RULET/AILET Score Card
  • B.Ed. programmes: PTET Scorecard and Counselling Allotment Letter

Financial Support

Scholarships &
Financial Aid

Where Merit Meets Opportunity — multiple government and institutional scholarship schemes available for eligible students.

🛵

Kalibai Bheel Medhavi Chhatra Scooty Yojana

For Female Students

Rajasthan Government scheme awarding free scooties to meritorious female students. Vinayak Girls College has a strong track record of students receiving this prestigious award.

Apply → hte.rajasthan.gov.in
🎓

National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

All Eligible Students

Central Government's unified scholarship portal — covering Pre-Matric, Post-Matric, and Merit-cum-Means scholarships for SC, ST, OBC, minority, and EWS students.

Apply → scholarships.gov.in
🏛️

Rajasthan State Scholarship Portal

Rajasthan Domicile Required

State government scholarships for SC/ST/OBC/EWS students enrolled in Rajasthan. Includes fee waivers, stipends, and hostel allowances for eligible categories.

Apply → rajpms.nic.in
📋

CM Higher Education Scholarship

BPL / EWS Families

Rajasthan Chief Minister's Higher Education Scholarship for BPL family students. Covers tuition fee and provides monthly stipend for eligible first-generation college students.

Apply → hte.rajasthan.gov.in
⚖️

SC/ST Fee Waivers

SC / ST Students

Complete or partial fee waiver for SC/ST category students as per University of Rajasthan and State Social Justice Department norms. Applied automatically upon category verification.

💼

OBC / EBC Scholarship

OBC / EBC Students

Rajasthan Social Justice & Empowerment Department scholarships for OBC and Economically Backward Class students. Applied via the Rajasthan State Scholarship Portal.

Apply → sje.rajasthan.gov.in
