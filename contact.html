FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

When do admissions open for 2026–27?

Admissions are currently open. Merit lists are generated after Board exam results are declared (typically May–July). LL.B. admissions are tied to CLAT/RULET/AILET result schedules.

Are hostel facilities available?

Yes. Secured, gender-specific hostels are available on campus. The Girls College and Girls Hostel have particularly strong safety protocols. Contact the administration office for hostel allocation.

What is the fee for LL.B.?

The total programme fee for the 3-year LL.B. at Vinayak Law College is ₹90,400 (approximately ₹30,133 per year). This covers tuition, examination, and institutional fees.

Can I apply for scholarships?

Yes. The institution actively facilitates NSP (National Scholarship Portal), Rajasthan State scholarships, SC/ST fee waivers, and the Kalibai Bheel Medhavi Chhatra Scooty Yojana for eligible female students.

Is Vinayak Girls College only for women?

Yes, Vinayak Girls P.G. College is exclusively for female students. It provides a completely safe and empowering environment staffed by PhD-qualified women faculty.

What is the NAAC grade of Vinayak International College?

Vinayak International College has been reaccredited with NAAC Grade A++ with a CGPA of 3.82 — among the highest scores in India in the 2017-18 assessment cycle.
