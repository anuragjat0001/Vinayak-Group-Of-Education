LL.B. — Bachelor of Laws
3-Year Professional Degree · BCI Approved · 120 Seats · Vinayak Law College
Apply Now

Programme Overview

Fully approved by the Bar Council of India and permanently affiliated with the University of Rajasthan, the 3-year LL.B. programme at Vinayak Law College is meticulously engineered for aspiring litigators, judges, and corporate counsels.

Vinayak Law College operates as a local monopoly — it is the only dedicated law college in the entire Chomu region, effectively capturing the total addressable market of local legal aspirants who would otherwise need to commute to central Jaipur.

Key Academic Features

  • State-of-the-Art Moot Court — Built to replicate the exact spatial and procedural environment of Indian judicial courts. Used for mock cross-examinations, case arguments, and adversarial debate training.
  • Dedicated Legal Library — Comprehensive archives of Supreme Court rulings, All India Reporter (AIR) volumes, and current law journals.
  • Judiciary Interactions — Management-facilitated interactions with sitting members of the judiciary, bridging academic law and real-world legal practice.
  • Court Visit Programmes — Mandatory visits to district courts and Rajasthan High Court providing first-hand legal environment exposure.
  • Intimate Faculty Ratio — 6 dedicated faculty serving approximately 171 students — ensuring personalized legal mentorship.

Career Pathways

  • Independent Litigation across District Courts & Rajasthan High Court
  • Rajasthan Judicial Services (RJS) — prestigious state judiciary examination
  • Corporate Legal Counsel / In-House Lawyer
  • Public Prosecution (PP) — District & High Court level
  • Legal Advisory roles in government departments
  • LLM / Advanced Legal Studies (for academia)

Admission Route: Students must register for and clear one of the following: CLAT (consortiumofnlus.ac.in), RULET (Rajasthan University Law Entrance Test), or AILET (nludelhi.ac.in). Admission is strictly based on entrance examination scores.
