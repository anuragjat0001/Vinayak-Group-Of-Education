Our Philosophy

Government Excellence.
Stable, Prestigious Careers.

A rigorous audit of career outcomes at the Vinayak Group reveals a clear and intentional strategic focus: the group is not designed for mass corporate campus recruitment. Instead, it serves as a comprehensive end-to-end pipeline for securing stable, prestigious government employment — perfectly aligned with the aspirations of students and families from the Chomu-Sikar-Jaipur corridor.

Graduates from Vinayak PG and Girls Colleges predominantly funnel their energies into state-level competitive examinations — and the results speak for themselves. The Vinayak Coaching Institute, operating with over 12,900 YouTube subscribers, provides ongoing academic support that continues even after graduation.

The Vinayak Career Advantage: Unlike institutions that offer vague "placement support," Vinayak builds a complete training ecosystem around specific, high-value government career targets — REET, RPSC Grade 2, RJS, Patwari, Banking — ensuring graduates know exactly which examination to prepare for and have dedicated coaching support to clear it.

The most potent, yet historically under-promoted, asset of the Vinayak Group is its parallel academic arm: the Vinayak Coaching Institute. This dedicated coaching facility provides intensive preparation for government examinations that are the primary career targets of Vinayak graduates.

The Institute operates a highly active YouTube channel — currently boasting over 12,900 subscribers — delivering grueling, multi-hour live-streamed coaching sessions specifically targeted at 2nd Grade Science and Chemistry government teaching examinations. This demonstrates the group's true USP: it is an end-to-end pipeline for government employment.

Career Pathways

Stream-Wise Career Outcomes

Specific career trajectories mapped by programme — from competitive exam targets to professional practice areas.

B.A. / M.A. Graduates

  • 🎯 REET Level 1 & 2 — Primary & Secondary School Teachers
  • 🎯 RPSC Grade 2 (Social Science) — Senior Teacher posts
  • 🎯 Rajasthan Patwari Exam — Revenue Department
  • 🎯 RAS (Rajasthan Admin Services) — State Civil Services
  • 🎯 SSC CGL / CHSL — Central Government posts
  • 🎯 IBPS PO / Clerk — Public Sector Banking
  • 🎯 NET / SET — College Lecturer positions

B.Sc. / M.Sc. Graduates

  • 🎯 REET Level 2 (Science) — Science Teacher posts (Primary target)
  • 🎯 RPSC Grade 2 (Science/Chemistry) — Senior Science Teachers
  • 🎯 CSIR-UGC NET JRF — Research Fellowship & Lectureship
  • 🎯 GATE — PSU recruitment & M.Tech admissions
  • 🎯 Biotechnology Industry — leverages DBT Star lab training
  • 🎯 Clinical Labs & Hospitals — Medical laboratory roles
  • 🎯 DRDO / ISRO / ICAR — Government research organizations

LL.B. Graduates — Law College

  • ⚖️ Independent Litigation — District Courts & Rajasthan High Court
  • ⚖️ Rajasthan Judicial Services (RJS) — Prestigious state judiciary exam
  • ⚖️ Public Prosecution — District and High Court PP posts
  • ⚖️ Corporate Legal Counsel — In-house legal roles
  • ⚖️ Government Legal Advisory — State department counsels
  • ⚖️ LLM / Judicial Academics — For those targeting legal education
  • ⚖️ UPSC Law Optional — IAS/IPS with law specialization

B.A.B.Ed. / B.Sc.B.Ed. Graduates

  • 🎓 REET Level 1 & 2 — Directly eligible upon graduation
  • 🎓 CTET (Central TET) — KVS, NVS, Central school teaching
  • 🎓 RPSC Grade 2 — Rajasthan Senior Teacher exams
  • 🎓 Vinayak Shiksha Samiti — Teaching roles within the group's 18 institutions
  • 🎓 Private School Teaching — CBSE/ICSE affiliated schools
  • 🎓 EdTech Content — Online education platform roles
