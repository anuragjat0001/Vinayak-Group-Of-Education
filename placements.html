  • 🎯 REET Level 2 Science — Primary Target
  • 🎯 RPSC Grade 2 Science/Chemistry

  • 🎯 DRDO / ISRO / ICAR — Govt. Research
  • 🎯 Clinical Labs — Medical Laboratory roles
