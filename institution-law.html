About

Chomu's Only
Dedicated Law College

Vinayak Law College operates as a local monopoly in legal education — it is the only dedicated, BCI-approved law college in the entire Chomu region. This unique positioning eliminates the traditional barrier for local legal aspirants: the necessity of commuting daily to central Jaipur for professional legal education.

The college is deliberately intimate in its design. With just 6 dedicated faculty members serving approximately 171 students, it maintains a highly favourable student-to-teacher ratio that enables the kind of personalized legal mentorship that large urban law colleges simply cannot offer.

The pedagogical focus is aggressive and practical: students are not merely taught legal theory — they are thrust into adversarial debates, mock trials, and high-pressure Moot Court environments from the very beginning of their legal education journey.

Evening Batch Available: Vinayak Law College also offers evening class batches, enabling working professionals and students with daytime commitments to pursue the LL.B. degree without compromising existing responsibilities.

Flagship Facility

The Moot Court Experience

Not a classroom. Not a seminar room. A full-scale judicial training environment — the most important infrastructure investment the college has made.

01

Authentic Judicial Architecture

The Moot Court replicates the spatial layout of actual Indian district courts — Judge's elevated bench, witness box, counsels' podiums, and gallery seating — creating an authentic psychological environment for legal training.

02

Judiciary Interaction Programme

Management facilitates direct interactions with sitting members of the Rajasthan judiciary — an extraordinary networking and mentorship opportunity that bridges the gap between academic legal theory and real-world judicial practice.

03

Court Visit Programmes

Mandatory, structured visits to District Courts and the Rajasthan High Court, Jaipur — giving students first-hand exposure to active legal proceedings, court etiquette, and the gritty realities of the Indian legal system.

How to Apply

LL.B. Admission
Process

  1. 1

    Register for Entrance Exam

    Register for CLAT (consortiumofnlus.ac.in), RULET (Rajasthan University), or AILET (nludelhi.ac.in). Admission is strictly based on entrance exam performance.

  2. 2

    Verify Eligibility

    Must hold a Graduation degree in any discipline with minimum 45% marks (General category). SC/ST/OBC — check for category-specific relaxations.

  3. 3

    Submit Application

    Apply online via this website or submit a physical application at the law college admission office, Chomu campus. Call 9414247984 for guidance.

  4. 4

    Document Verification

    Attend document verification with all original graduation certificates, TC, ID proof, and entrance exam scorecard.

  5. 5

    Pay Fee & Enrol

    Pay ₹90,400 total programme fee in instalments as per college schedule. Seat is confirmed upon fee payment. Join the Moot Court from Day 1.
