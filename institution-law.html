Flagship Facility

The Moot Court
Experience

No legal education is complete without the visceral experience of standing before a court. Our Moot Court facility replicates the exact spatial and psychological environment of India's district courts — training your reflexes, your voice, and your composure for the real thing.

Witness Box & Cross-Examination
Students take turns as counsel and witnesses, developing cross-examination skills and thinking under pressure.

Court Visit Programme
