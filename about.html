Our History

From a Humble Trust to
Rajasthan's Pride

The Vinayak Group traces its lineage from the Vinayak Shiksha Samiti — a philanthropic educational trust with deep roots in the Chomu-Sikar educational belt of Rajasthan. The Samiti, with an educational legacy stretching back over a century, began modernizing its higher education operations in 2004 with the establishment of Vinayak PG College.

What started as a co-educational undergraduate college on a then-modest campus has, through strategic vertical integration, evolved into a comprehensive "grand Banyan tree" — offering cradle-to-career educational pathways from playgroup schooling to postgraduate degrees and professional legal education.

Group Tagline

"Where Learning Meets Legacy"

Pre-2004
Vinayak Shiksha Samiti — The Legacy Trust
Ancestral philanthropic educational trust established in the Chomu-Sikar belt, providing foundational schooling across the region for generations.
2004
Vinayak PG College Founded
The flagship co-educational degree college established on Kacholiya Road, Chomu, permanently affiliated with the University of Rajasthan, Jaipur. Marks the formal entry into higher education.
2007
Vinayak Girls P.G. College — Women's Empowerment
Recognizing the critical need for female higher education in semi-urban Rajasthan, a dedicated women's college is established to provide a safe, empowering environment for girl students.
2013
Vinayak International College & Law College Founded
Aggressive horizontal expansion. The elite International College (later NAAC A++) and the groundbreaking Vinayak Law College (BCI approved — Chomu's only law institution) are established.
2017–18
NAAC A++ Reaccreditation — 3.82 CGPA
A watershed moment: Vinayak International College achieves NAAC A++ reaccreditation with a remarkable 3.82 CGPA — among the highest scores in India, validating decades of quality commitment.
2018
Vinayak International School — CBSE Affiliation
The complete feeder ecosystem is completed with the establishment of a CBSE-affiliated English-medium school with DigiBoard smart classrooms, swimming pool, and day-cum-boarding facilities.
2020–21
National & Media Recognition
Ranked #1 in Rajasthan by The Week magazine (2020). Ranked #3 in India for Good Management Practices by Education World Grand Jury Awards (2021). NIRF Rank 81 confirmed by Ministry of Education.
Today
32,000+ Students Across 18 Institutions
The Vinayak Group now operates a comprehensive multi-institutional ecosystem on 50+ acres, serving over 32,000 students annually across the Chomu-Sikar-Jaipur educational corridor.

Recognition

Accreditations, Rankings
& National Honours

The Vinayak Group holds a portfolio of accreditations that places it in an elite tier of India's private educational sector — virtually unmatched in the semi-urban Rajasthan landscape.

A++
NAAC Reaccreditation
National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC-UGC) re-accreditation with a CGPA of 3.82 — secured by Vinayak International College during the 2017-18 assessment cycle. The institution asserts this is among the highest scores in India for a college in its category, making it a Tier-1 academic credential in the Rajasthan market.
#81
NIRF India Ranking
Ranked 81st in the "India Rankings 2021: College" category by the National Institutional Ranking Framework, administered by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. The group maintained its position among the top 200 colleges in previous ranking cycles — a remarkable achievement for a semi-urban institution.
DBT★
DBT Star College Scheme
Elevated to the prestigious DBT Star College status under the scheme administered by the Department of Biotechnology, Government of India. This award signifies exceptional infrastructure for life sciences and scientific research, enabling cutting-edge undergraduate research programmes.
BCI
Bar Council of India Approval
Vinayak Law College is formally approved by the Bar Council of India (BCI) for its 3-year LL.B. programme (120 seats). This is the mandatory national approval for law degrees in India, ensuring all graduates hold nationally recognized professional legal qualifications.
NCTE
NCTE Approval — Teacher Training
The integrated teacher education programmes — B.A.B.Ed. and B.Sc.B.Ed. — are sanctioned by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE), ensuring that all graduates receive nationally valid teaching qualifications recognized for REET, CTET, and government school appointments.
UoR
University of Rajasthan Affiliation
All primary higher education institutions — Vinayak PG College, Vinayak International College, and Vinayak Law College — are permanently affiliated with the University of Rajasthan (UNIRAJ), Jaipur, ensuring that all degrees awarded are government-recognized and universally valid across India.
#1
The Week — Rajasthan Rank
Ranked 1st in Rajasthan in a 2020 national-level survey conducted by The Week magazine. This independent media assessment validates the institution's position as Rajasthan's premier private college group outside major metro areas.
#3
Education World Grand Jury Awards
Ranked 3rd across all of India for "Good Management Practices & Systems" by the Education World Grand Jury Awards 2021 — a testament to the group's institutional governance, administrative efficiency, and consistent quality delivery.
