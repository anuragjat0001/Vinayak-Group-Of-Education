Our Story

Where Learning
Meets Legacy

The Vinayak Group of Institutions is the flagship academic arm of Vinayak Shiksha Samiti — a trust founded by Shri Navrang Choudhary with one singular ambition: to make world-class education accessible to students from Chomu and the surrounding Shekhawati-Jaipur corridor, without forcing them to migrate to expensive city campuses.

What began in 2004 as Vinayak PG College has grown into a comprehensive group of five institutions — all permanently affiliated with the University of Rajasthan, Jaipur, and collectively serving over 32,000 students across UG, PG, law, and school programmes.

Today, Vinayak International College carries the prestigious NAAC A++ (3.82 CGPA) reaccreditation and NIRF Rank 81 — benchmarks that stand alongside the very best colleges in India.

Tagline: "Where Learning Meets Legacy" — a commitment to both academic rigour and the enduring values of character, discipline, and service.

Journey

Milestones on the Path to Excellence

2004

Vinayak PG College Established

First institution of the group founded on Kacholiya Road, Chomu. Affiliated to University of Rajasthan from inception.

2007

Vinayak Girls P.G. College

Dedicated women's college established, addressing the acute gap in safe higher education for girls in the rural Chomu-Sikar region.

2013

International College + Law College

Two landmark institutions opened simultaneously — Vinayak International College (now NAAC A++) and Vinayak Law College (BCI Approved, first in Chomu).

2017–18

NAAC A++ Reaccreditation

Vinayak International College achieves NAAC A++ with 3.82 CGPA — among the highest for any private institution in Rajasthan.

2018

Vinayak International School

CBSE-affiliated English-medium day-cum-boarding school launched with 20 DigiBoard smart classrooms — completing the K-12 to postgrad pipeline.

2020–21

National Rankings & DBT Star

#1 in Rajasthan (The Week), #3 in India for Good Management (Education World), NIRF Rank 81 (Ministry of Education), and DBT Star College designation — a historic quadruple.

2026

32,000+ Students, 18 Institutions

The Vinayak Shiksha Samiti now oversees 18 institutions across the Chomu-Sikar region, with 32,000+ students and a growing research and competitive exam coaching infrastructure.

Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the foremost institution of academic excellence in rural Rajasthan — one that produces not merely degree-holders, but disciplined, ethical, and capable individuals who contribute meaningfully to society and the nation.

Our Mission

To deliver affordable, high-quality education across all disciplines — with faculty, infrastructure, and character-building programmes that match or exceed institutions in India's major cities, while remaining rooted in the values and aspirations of local communities.

Our Values

Discipline. Integrity. Academic Rigour. Physical Wellness. Social Responsibility. We believe that education without character is incomplete — and that the most valuable asset a student leaves with is not a certificate, but an unbreakable work ethic.

Born and raised in the Sikar district of Rajasthan, Shri Navrang Choudhary built a successful career in business and real estate before turning his entrepreneurial vision toward education — driven by the belief that geographical location should never be a barrier to quality academic opportunity.
